The Venue

Chimera will run at Motu Moana Scout Camp, in Green Bay, Auckland. It is located in Connaught Street and is 3km from New Lynn Transport Centre and Lynn Mall.

Catering on site

Meals for Saturday and Sunday will be catered. Breakfast will be $5 and dinners and lunches will be $7 per meal. Soft drinks and candy will be available also.

We are pleased to offer a special pre-pay weekend meal price: the whole weekend's meals for $25 if you pay before August 1st. Not only is this a saving of $6 off the full price, but it means you don't have to worry about cash on the day, and get to jump to the head of line!

Gluten free, vegetarian and vegan options are available. Please email us to let us know your requirements.

Costs

Chimera will cost as follows:

  • Full Convention - $55 (a $5 discount is available for nzLARPS members, as well as a $5 discount for payment before August 1st. These discounts stack)
  • One Day Only - $25 (no discounts apply)
  • A Town Called Refuge only - $10 (no discounts apply)

Accommodation

There are 46 beds available at the venue. Accommodation is clean scout bunks, with access to bathroom, shower and kitchen facilities. Accommodation is free of charge but priority will be given for those coming from out of Auckland.

Prizes

Prizes will be awarded for three major categories: Best Written Game, Best Roleplayer, and Best Costuming. These categories voted on across the whole convention by the players and GMs.

There will also be a prize of Best Roleplayer and Best Costume (Male) and Best Costume (Female) for just A Town Called Refuge. And of course, heaps of spot prizes just for being there!
