Costs

Chimera will cost as follows:

  • Full Convention - $55
  • One Day Only - $25 (no discounts apply)
  • A Town Called Refuge only - $10 (no discounts apply)
Prepayed catering - $25 if you pay before August 1st.

Discounts

If you take the "Full Convention" option there is a $5 discount if you pay before August 1st, and a further $5 discount if you are a member of nzLARPS.

Bed Space

There are 46 beds available at the venue. Accommodation is clean scout bunks, with access to bathroom, shower and kitchen facilities. Accommodation is free of charge but priority will be given to those coming from out of Auckland.
