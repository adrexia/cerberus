GAME SELECTION

Note: we're filling up quickly, so we're going to put on extra games on Friday, Saturday morning and afternoon, and Sunday morning. These will be added to this form on Thursday 2 June, and will consist of a mix of scenarios from overseas and re-runs of games that we already have listed. Feel free to make your selection now anyway, and note under Preferences in each round if you want to have to option of swapping to one of the new games.

Have you registered?

Before selecting your games, please ensure you have registered for the convention.

The Preferences field

Under "Preferences" for each round, please note which characters you would like to play in each of your choices. If you want to go on a waiting list to play in your first choice of game, let us know that too. If none of the options available in a round really appeal to you, make a note of that and we'll see what we can do.

A Town Called Refuge

After you complete this form, if you are attending A Town Called Refuge then you will be asked to fill in a questionnaire about your preferences for that game.

Your selection

When making your selections, please do not select the same game as both first and second choice. We will do our best to place you in each of your preferred events. If an event is over-booked, we will be in contact to discuss options.
