Background

1980's Texas is all about money and oil. It doesn't just matter who you are, but how much you are worth. The women are all beautiful with big hair, big smiles and big shoulder pads. Everything is larger than life. The older man is in vogue, they're the ones with the money. The women around here may sometimes be loose but they are definitely not free.

Tonight is a lavish birthday party for R.J. Harrington held at his parents' ranch - SouthSpoon. Family and friends are gathered to wish R.J. well on his 30th birthday and coming of age. As he turns 30, R.J. comes into the inheritance left to him by his grandfather Eli Harrington.
