Nexus: Gathering at Foxlight

Authors

Cameron McLean, Jason Farley, Justin Marsh, Patrick Cummuskey and Peter Cummuskey

Characters

There are 17 characters.

Athena (F) Member of the Cavaliers of Trentham cabal. A beautiful young mage with an aptitude for life magic and an interest in helping humanity achieve its potential.

Aurora (F) Member of the Magnificent Seven cabal. A mage skilled in the art of weaving wards and protections, with a great concern about the defences of the city.

Carvewood (M) Member of the Watchers cabal. Renowned explorer and collector of artefacts and mysterious items, and a mage adept in matter manipulation.

Cassandra (F) Member of the Dawning Tide cabal. Like her namesake, she is a diviner of the future and a master of the temporal magics.

Dante (M) Member of the Watchers cabal. Prone to indulging in his wrathful nature, he is a consummate practitioner of fiery magics and a staunch advocate for aggressive action against threats.

Dartford (M) Member of the Voidwalker cabal. A psychologist first and foremost with a keen understanding of the magic of the mind, and a wish to see enlightenment spread throughout the world.

Earl White (M) Member of the Cavaliers of Trentham cabal. Adept with both the blade and magic that lies within the arcanum of forces.

Lucius (M) Member of the Ashstone cabal. A cool and calculating mage who is always looking at the big picture, with a capable understanding of space magic to assist that perspective.

Miranda (F) Member of the Ashstone cabal. Sensuous and determined with an eye for style and a keen understanding of mental magic and manipulation.

Persephone (F) Member of the Ashstone cabal. Despite her grim appearance she is a friendly mage whose use of necromantic magic serves to keep her circle of friends quite small.

Prism (F) Member of the Cavaliers of Trentham cabal. An experienced mage with an eye to unifying the mages of the city, and a master of the prime magic arcanum.

Rohan (M) Member of the Magnificent Seven cabal. A gunslinger with a smattering of different magics and a humorous take on life and other matters.

Summerthaw (M) Member of the Dawning Tide cabal. Good-natured yet trickster-like at times he is adept with understanding the magic of fate.

Spectre (M) Member of the Magnificent Seven cabal. A warrior dedicated to the defence of the Covenant who is strengthened by ghosts that he has bound to his service.

Von Himmel (M) Member of the Voidwalker cabal. Slightly odd in his mannerisms, possibly due to the significant interaction he has with spirits and otherworldly entities.

We will not be using be standard WoD rules. A light resolution system will be posted in due course, but this game will be quite rules light.


Background

It is 1943, and mages of the Covenant of Auckland are gathering at Foxlight Chancery to socialise and discuss matters of importance amidst the continuing pressures of WW2. Amongst the business there will be the reswearing of oaths of allegiance to the Covenant and the resolution of some minor matters of dispute. The matter of recent encounters with apparently Nazi mages is also a great cause for concern amongst the attendees, although the upcoming wedding of two of the assembled mages has done a lot to keep spirits buoyed.
