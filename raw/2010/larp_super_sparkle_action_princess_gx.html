Costume

The 'cast' will need to bring their costumes for filming (Tiara/Ti'ina will need two outfits to show the transformation, it would help if the Versatile Extra brought several changes, although there will be a costume box supplied for some extra changes.) The 'crew' should bring modern day clothes suitable to a film set. Some of this game will take place outside - please don't wear anything you'd hate to get dirty.

Background

You are the cast and crew of TV's hottest new children's show: Super Sparkle Action Princess GX! It's got a ballerina turned action heroine, evil cyborg aliens, sparkly transformation scenes and a lot of riding around on hoverbikes. The kiddies love that stuff. But today there's a problem: your footage for this week's episode disappeared in a freak giraffe incident, the script is a collection of incoherent jottings from your drunken absentee writer, and the props mistress hates you. Your deadline is today, and failure is not an option. It won't just get you fired, it'll get you a really nasty phone call from your five year old niece. Are you a trouper?
