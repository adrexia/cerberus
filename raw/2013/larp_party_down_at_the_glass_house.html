Background

Glass House is the hottest show on TV right now. The house mates are all young, hot, and were chosen by the producers for their gigantic egos, outrageous personalities and their thirst for fame. The make ups, the break ups, the fights, the hook ups - all of this is an equation for the highest rating show on TV right now.

But tonight the stakes are raised: the whole purpose of this house party episode is to throw in the biggest change yet. Today one member of the house will get voted out, and one of the house guests will get voted in.

The house mates have all been allowed to invite one person from home to the party, and four lucky fans of the show have won entry. In this epic quest for fame, what's going to go down on tonight's episode of Glass House?

Note: This game will feature use of a video camera in an adjoining room as a 'confessions' room and the resulting in-game confessions will become a part of the post-game breakdown
