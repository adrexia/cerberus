CHIMERA - Live Action Roleplaying Convention - 15th - 17th August 2014

Chimera 2014...Coming Soon!

Seventh Annual Chimera Larp Convention: 15th-17th August Auckland, New Zealand

The website for Chimera 2014 will be available 6pm, Saturday 24th May 2014. It will include all the information you need to plan your Chimera weekend, including timetables, game information and catering options.

Our lineup this year includes brand new games by New Zealand writers, overseas games, and games from other events being rerun due to popular demand.

The Saturday night of Chimera 2014 is split across three big larping events, including a brand new 60 player game being written especially for Chimera 2014.

Welcome

Last year's convention was a huge success, with 180 people from around New Zealand attending the weekend!

Chimera 2009 was also featured on 20/20, you can watch the article here.

We are now pleased to present the seventh annual New Zealand live action role playing convention, Chimera 2014. It's going to be a fantastic weekend, so make sure you don't miss out.
