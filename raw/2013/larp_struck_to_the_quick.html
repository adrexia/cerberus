Costume

The majority of characters in this game are dressed in casual clothing, although they each have their own individual styles. Jeans and T-shirts are more than acceptable; your costume should not prevent you from playing tag (not that we'll be doing that, it's just the best analogy I could think of)

Background

Raumati High School's seventh-form drama class has developed such a reputation after their productions of 'A Midsummer Night's Dream' and 'Twelfth Night' that they've been invited to the Sheilah Winn Shakespeare Festival. This version of 'The Tempest' might not go as smoothly as anyone hoped, though, least of all because you somehow wound up stranded at a truck stop with no cellphone reception.

'Struck to the Quick' is the third and final part of the 'Quick' trilogy. Russ Kale is the author of 'Angels in the Fog'. William Shakespeare is buried in Stratford-Upon-Avon.
