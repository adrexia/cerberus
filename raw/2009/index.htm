JOIN US FOR A WEEKEND OF LARP
21-23 AUGUST 2009, AUCKLAND, NEW ZEALAND
Live action role playing is taking off in New Zealand, and Chimera is riding the wave. This year we're offering a veritable smorgasbord of scenarios. It doesn't matter if you've never larped before, because all of our larps are designed to be quick to pick up.

You'll become Victorian ladies and gentlemen, legendary knights and damsels, spies, dimensional travellers, wizards, soap opera stars, vampires, and more. Friday night kicks off with horror scenarios, on Saturday there are two rounds of games followed by The Great Exhibition with 60+ players, and on Sunday there are another two full rounds.
