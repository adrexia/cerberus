Premise
It came from the heavens. For centuries it lay dormant beneath the ground, hidden by an ancient shaman, in the hope it would never see the light of day again.

And he was right... until now. The year is 1925 and you are a student from the Miskatonic University, visiting a mining camp in Pennsylvania.
