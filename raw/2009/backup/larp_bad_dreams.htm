BACKGROUND
In this world there are powerful people, the movers and the shakers, those who rule through manipulation, and then there’s you.

For centuries your organisation has ruled and manipulated the entire world. Your powers are beyond the imagination of ordinary people and beyond the dreams of the most savvy power grabbers. You are the grandmaster of one of the most secret orders of the world.

So how is it that someone managed to deliver a letter directly to you, inviting you to a gathering you know nothing about? There is nothing in this world of which you do not know about! It is time for you to face the unknown, could there possibly be anyone in this world more powerful than you are?
