FEEDBACK
Thank you for attending Chimera. We value your opinions and thoughts, and would love to know what we can do to improve for next year's event. Please take the time to look over the following questionnaire, and answer some of the questions. The forms are anonymous, and you don't have to answer all the questions. All feedback is greatly appreciated.
Venue
How did you find the venue? Was it appropriate for the event? If it was inappropriate, what alternative venues would you suggest we look into?
Catering
How did you find the onsite catering? Would you prefer an event with food provided in the convention cost, an event where you have to go offsite for food, or an event like this Chimera where you had the option to go offsite or stay onsite? If you didn't buy the onsite food, what would you like to see in onsite catering that would make you want to buy food?
Event Length
How did you find the event length? Would you prefer Friday-Saturday event in future, or continue with the Friday-Sunday format?
Games
How did you find the selection of games on offer? Too many games? Not enough? Are there particular kinds of games you'd like to see that we didn't offer?
Price
How did you find the price of the event?
Registration and game selection
Was registration and game selection an easy process? Do you have any suggestions for improvements on registration/game selection?
Organisers
Were the organisers easy to approach for help before and during the event?
Overall opinion
What was your overall opinion of the event?
Next year
Are you intending to return to Chimera next year, providing all other conditions are favourable? If not, what is the biggest factor in your decision to not return? Is this an event you'd try and convince your friends to attend?
Other
Do you have any other feedback on things you enjoyed and liked about the event, or things that you'd like to see improved on, or any other suggestions at all?
