Cast

Costume
Weapons will be available to borrow and costume by way of tabards will be provided. Suitably supportive shoes are a must.
Rules
Combat will be with foam weapons. A variety of rules may be used depending on the scenarios played.
GM contact

Background
Looking to try your hand at larp combat, brush up on your skills or just spend a low-key afternoon laying waste to your enemies?

The Brawl will be a mixture of combat games ranging from Capture the Flag to Orcball, all about high adrenaline, pure combat and learning the tactics of larp fighting.

Grab a sword, pick up a shield, heft an axe and join in the carnage.
