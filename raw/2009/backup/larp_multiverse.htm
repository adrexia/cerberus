Background
Based on Michael Moorcock’s Eternal Champion cycle, Multiverse is high fantasy adventure set against a backdrop of war amongst the gods themselves.

Players undertake quests at the behest of their chosen gods, becoming key agents in age-old clash between Law and Chaos.
