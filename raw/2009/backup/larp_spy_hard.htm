Background
Looking for romance, thrills and adventure? It’s 1969 (oh behave!) and your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to infiltrate the party of the decade. A party riddled with secret agents, double agents, defecting scientists, super villains, henchmen and seductive sirens. Achieve your mission, seduce the double agent and save (or destroy) the world. All without spilling your martini. Spy Hard: Danger! Suspense! Excitement! It’s the party with it all.

Due to the excessive dancing, adult content and sexual innuendo this event is strictly R18.
