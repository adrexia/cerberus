TIMETABLE
Friday
Chimera will kick off at 7:30pm with an informal Meet and Greet in the main hall. There will be some nibbles and drinks provided. This is a chance to meet some of the new people and catch up with old friends before the larping kicks off. At 9pm, the first round of games will begin, featuring three horror larps and one horror alternative - an action-comedy James Bond spoof. Friday will wrap up at midnight.
Saturday
Breakfast will be available from 8:30am on site. The second round of games will start at 10am sharp, all players and GMs will need to muster at the registration table at 9:50am. The games will run until 1pm, at which point there will be a one hour break for costume change and lunch. Lunch will be available on site. The third round will start at 2pm and all players and GMs are required to muster at 1:50pm. This round comes to a finish at 5pm. Dinner will be available on site until 6:30pm. Briefing for round four, The Great Exhibition, will start at 7:30pm and the game proper will begin at 8pm. The evening, complete with clean up and debrief, is expected to wind up at midnight.
Sunday
Breakfast will be available from 8:30am on site. The fifth round of games will start at 10am sharp, all players and GMs will need to muster at the registration table at 9:50am. The games will run until 1pm, at which point there will be a one hour break for costume change and lunch. Lunch will be available on site. The sixth round will start at 2pm and all players and GMs are required to muster at 1:50pm. This round comes to a finish at 5pm. Everyone is expected to help clean up the venue and we expect to be ready to leave at 6pm. Informal post-Chimera dinner/drinks is a possibility, if any of us are still standing by this stage in the weekend.
