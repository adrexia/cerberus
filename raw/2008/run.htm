Games must run as close to three hours as possible, and characters must be pre-written. Your game should probably require less than 30 people to run. Ideally, action should be contained to one room or area, but this can be indoors or outdoors.

If selected, games must be completely written by the 1st of August 2008 or they will be removed from the lineup.

If you're interested in giving it a go, write an outline of your idea and send it in to chimera@nzlarps.org.

We are able to assist in providing props and gear, so talk to us about what you'll need and we'll see what we can arrange.

We will endeavor to run as many games as possible, but we are limited by time and the number of player registrations we receive.
