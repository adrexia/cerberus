The New Zealand Live Action Role Playing Society was pleased to host CHIMERA, a live roleplaying convention that brought together larps and larpers from around the country to enjoy a weekend packed with games.

The inaugural Chimera Live Action Roleplaying Convention was successfully run on 22nd-24th August 2008, and had over 70 people attend over two days. If you missed it this year, don't worry - stay tuned for forthcoming information on Chimera 2009
