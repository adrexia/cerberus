Venue

Chimera will run at Motu Moana Scout Camp, in Green Bay, Auckland. It is located in Connaught Street and is 3km from New Lynn Transport Centre and Lynn Mall.

Accomodation: We are able to offer free accommodation for 35 people at the venue, and priority is given to attendees from outside Auckland. Accommodation is clean scout bunks, with access to bathroom, shower and kitchen facilities. Free accommodation is allocated on a first in, first served basis. Once the 35 free beds are booked, accommodation is available for $10 a night.

Food: Steve Shipway will be providing cooked meals during the convention. Breakfast will be available both mornings from 9am-10am for $5, lunch and dinner will be available on Saturday $7 (each meal) There will also be chocolate and cans of soft drink for sale at all times during the convention. Weekend food tickets are available at a rate of $30 (5 meals + 2 cans of soft drink) or the Serenity player option for $27 (4 meals and one can of soft drink). For more information, please click here.

Road Map: smaps.co.nz

Public Transport Information: www.maxx.co.nz

Since the venue is a little out of the way, carpooling is advised.
