Breakout Day

Written by Morgan Davie, Zak Clark, Luke Walker, Jonathan Todd and Nate Cull

It's "breakout day" for the call centre staff of Zonix Inc, an evening devoted to training and teambuilding, to combat flagging morale and help the employees 'breakout' of their ordinary routine and get the whole staff humming! But there are a few concerning things - there's an efficiency expert on site, rumours of pending layoffs, and no one who works for Zonix Inc. actually knows what product they sell.

Still, the evening is paid, and you'll get to meet some of the staff from the other team. Just stay on your toes, try to look useful in front of the efficiency expert and everything will be fine. After all, there can't be anything too weird going on, right?
