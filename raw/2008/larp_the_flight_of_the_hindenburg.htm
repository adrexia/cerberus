The Flight of the Hindenburg

Written by the KapCon XVI Larp Team

It's the 1930s, and you are a guest aboard the airship Hindenberg, the largest aircraft to ever fly. For the airship's premiere flight from New York a special charity gala is being hosted to raise money for local shelters and soup kitchens. Expect gangsters, dashing heroes, Nazis, glamorous stars and much, much more!

The Flight of the Hindenburg accomodates up to 65 players, and will run on the Saturday night of Chimera as the centrepiece.
