Orcball - The Chimera Cup

Orcball is the sport that combines the thrill of team sports with the adrenaline of boffer weapons. Teams of five will compete in ten minute games to score the most points, competing to win the first Chimera Cup!

The Rules

Please keep an eye on this space, the official rules for Chimera Orcball will be posted soon.

The Teams

Participants are invited to put together teams of their own and submit them on the game choice form, or by contacting the Co-Ordinator. Individuals may opt to be randomly placed in a team.

Costuming Requirements

None. Generic fantasy is a bonus, good running shoes are a must.
