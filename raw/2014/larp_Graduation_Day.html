Costume

Graduation Day is set in a fairly generic fantasy world (wizards). The feast occurs in the great towerof the White College in the independent city-state of Tourmaline.


Background

The independent city-state of Tourmaline is ruled by the wizards of the White College. Prince Ambrose, the younger son of the King of neighbouring Arlandia, was sent to the White College to receive a gentleman's education. To the surprise of many he actually passed his final exams. Today Prince Ambrose is holding the traditional graduation feast to honour the College Masters.

Graduation day is a theatreform LARP designed for 12 players. The scene is the graduation day feast of Prince Ambrose who is graduating from the White College, a university for wizards in the city of Tourmaline.
