TIMETABLE

Friday

Chimera will kick off at 6pm with an informal meet and greet in the main hall. This is a chance to meet some of the new people and catch up with old friends before the larping kicks off. Chimera officially opens at 7:30pm with the first muster. At 8pm, the first round of games will begin. Friday will wrap up at 11pm.

Saturday

Breakfast will be available from 8:30am on site. The second round of games will start at 10am sharp, all players and GMs will need to muster at the Muster Point (outside the middle hall) at 9:50am. The games will run until 1pm, at which point there will be a one hour break for costume change and lunch. Lunch will be available on site. The third round will start at 2pm and all players and GMs are required to muster at 1:50pm. This round comes to a finish at 5pm. Dinner will be available on site until 6:00pm. Briefing for round four, the flagship round, will start at 7:00pm in the Mowgli building (the building at the top of the site) and the game proper will begin at 7:30pm. The evening, complete with prize-giving and debrief, is expected to wind up at midnight.

Sunday

Breakfast will be available from 8:30am on site. The fifth round of games will start at 10am sharp, all players and GMs will need to muster at the registration table at 9:50am. The games will run until 1pm, at which point there will be a one hour break for costume change and lunch. Lunch will be available on site. The sixth round will start at 2pm and all players and GMs are required to muster at 1:50pm. This round comes to a finish at 5pm. Everyone is expected to help clean up the venue and we expect to be ready to leave at 6pm.
