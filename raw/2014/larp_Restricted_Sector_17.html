Restricted Sector 17

Authors

Dave Agnew, Claire Ahuriri, Sarah Daymond, Jordan Henare, Mark Wilson.

Characters

20 Players, plus crew spots. All characters are gender neutral.

Costume

Military-SciFi + Nerf guns. You must bring your own nerf guns.

Setting

Set in the Mage: Fight For Survival universe. No prior knowledge of the rules or setting is required.


Background

Somewhere in the depths of space, an ancient artifact has been discovered. Teams from each of the five supernations are racing towards their objective, each aiming to reach it before their rivals, and to claim it for their own. However, all is not as it seems...

A Sci-Fi Action/Horror live combat (Nerf and boffer) game.
