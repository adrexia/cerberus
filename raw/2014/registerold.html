REGISTRATION

Chimera 2014 Registration and Game Selection will open at 7pm on Monday 26th May!

Before you register

To register for Chimera 2014, please fill in the form below. Before you get started, there's a few important things to note:

  • Please fill in your real name and correct information. We only need this information to be able to get in touch with you for the event, and to help look after you while you are there.
  • Chimera is largely an R16 event with attendees under 16 only allowed by special arrangement. If you are under 16, you and your Mum or Dad will need to get in touch with us via email. We categorically do not allow anyone under 13 but those between 13-16 may be able to attend with a parent or other adult.
  • Those who select on site accommodation will be contacted in July to arrange rooms. Those are able to stay off site are asked to do so to make room for out of towners, or those with no transport options. If you are able to billet friends at your residence for the convention, it would be great if you could. We are expecting to have greater accommodation demand than we have capacity.
  • For your first, second and third choices, please be sure to put different games. Putting the same game in all three slots does not increase your chances of getting that game. If you put the same game in more than one choice, the second two choices will be considered 'Lucky Dip'


Costs

Chimera will cost as follows:

  • Full Convention (5-6 games) - $70
  • Three/Four games - $55
  • Two Games - $30 (no discounts apply)
  • One Game only - $15 (no discounts apply)
  • Full Convention Lucky Dip - $30 (no discounts apply)
  • Prepaid Catering - $30 (no discounts)

Discounts

If you take the "Full Convention" or "Three/Four Games" option there is a $5 discount if you pay before July 15th, and a further $5 discount if you are a member of nzLARPS.

Bed Space

There are 84 beds available at the venue. Accommodation is clean scout bunks, with access to bathroom, shower and kitchen facilities. Accommodation is free of charge but priority will be given to those coming from out of Auckland.
