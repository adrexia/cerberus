The Soap and the Opera

Authors

Daniel Carleton, Saskia Rose, Jordan Henare and Serena Owen

Characters

For around 25 players.

Costume

Costume briefs are given with character sheets. They will not be overlu elaborate, mostly modern clothes.

Content Warning

The game will be high drama, consisting of plots along the lines murder, affairs, possible homosexuality and (pretend) violence. While the game will maintain a PG rating, if you are uncomfortable being involved in certain plots please contact the GMs.


Background

Scandals, murdering ex-lovers, backstabbing, love triangles, coming out of the closet...all in days work for a soap opera star, or a person whose life is a soap opera. Join the cast of The Soap and the Opera, set in Sunshine Bay, where anything can and will happen for the poor individuals trapped to live a life of constant drama. Players will take the role of one of the main characters in the soap, but with no script who knows what will happen!

Should your character meet its untimely end, don't worry! They likely had an identical twin sibling they never mentioned, or a friend wanting revenge or maybe a long lost lover tracing the footsteps of your previous character. The drama will never stop!
