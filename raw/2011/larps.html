THE LARPS

Asterix and the Council of Carnutes

By Jared Hansen

The Council of Carnutes has summoned Vitalstatistix demanding to know how and why his village is still resisting the Romans when the rest of the Gauls have stopped. The Council has summoned them to a secret location near the Forest of Carnutes, on the border of Gaul and Germania. Who knows what other adventures await the indomitable Gauls on the way? Find out more

Bad Dreams: Hurricane Hotel

By Ivan "Vanya" Essin, Gaffy Sandford, Porl Bowdler, Walter Hamer.

Outside the weather's frightful, but the fire's so delightful... A group of guests have gathered together for dinner put on by the generous host of their hotel, all are hiding from the storm of the century raging beyond the doors. Yet, most frightening of all, news of a serial killer passing through this small town has everyone afraid to be alone. Still, there is nothing to fear, after all, as long as everyone is together, nothing could possibly go wrong... Find out more

A Ball of Blood and Tears

By Garth Barley

It is the time of year when the council of the Ateh UkIL come together and make decisions on important matters. It is a time when old enemies to settle differences and romancers burst into new loves. But after the last war and the nearing of the end of the Zarl Dynasty, an arch nemesis arrives under a cape of friendship, and a stormcloud of chaos fast approaches. Find out more

The Board of Inquiry

By Kirsten Goodall and Chris Hoggins

A valuable scientific research facility in far-off Auckland, New Zealand, has been overrun and destroyed by supernatural elements. A board of inquiry is called to investigate this appalling lapse of security and interrogate the lone survivor. Find out more

Boffo's Birthday Bash

By Donna Giltrap

Cyril (Boffo) Boffingham is celebrating his birthday in style. He has invited an assortment of fellow members of the Drones Club and other good eggs to help him mark the occasion. Unfortunately a few blighters and an unavoidable Aunt or two always manage to make their way onto the guest list. There's also the small matter that Cyril needs to get married within the next 12 months in order to inherit the family fortune. A game in the style of P.G. Wodehouse. Find out more

The Ch'akta Peace Accord

By Nick O'Keefe

The Ch'akta peace accord was signed five years ago, ending a 38 year war across space between the Terran Colonies and the Ch'akta of Gilese 581b. The government of the Terran Colonies has decided that enough time has been spent on negotiations and discussions to allow delegates from the Ch'akta to visit Earth herself in a celebration of the original peace accord signed those five years ago. This spectacular event will be hosted at the New Washington War Memorial Museum. This will be the most momentous event of 23XX since the end of the war! Find out more

Colonel Sebastian T. Rawhide's Circus of the Spectacular

By Mike Young, run by Michelle Burt

Join Colonel Sebastian T. Rawhide and his Circus of the Spectacular as they return to America from their triumphant tour of Europe where they performed for kings and heads of state. But now the Circus is ailing financially. Can the valiant members of the Circus save the show from the clutches of the nefarious Bungling Brothers? Find out more

Corbett and Grimes, Private Detectives

By Joseph Outram

Classic 1940s noir television show, Corbett and Grimes, Private Detectives has been revived by New Zealand's Sky network, and the smash hit series is one episode away from completion. Unfortunately, on the filming of this crucial season finale, everything seems to be going wrong! Will the cast and crew of Corbett and Grimes manage to come through? Or will the show fade back into obscurity? Find out more

The Defence of Nurumburg

By Adam Parsons

Each spring from the northern wastes pour waves of raiding savages into the peaceful farm lands of the Empire. Rumour has it that the great city of Praag has fallen to the invaders who now march into the heart of the Empire. Plundering and looting as they go. The small village of Nurumburg believes itself safe from the chaotic tide flowing south however the Viscount has ordered that all able bodied persons are to be trained as a militia force to aid in the defence of the province! Men-at-arms from the Viscount's own army have been dispatched to assist in the training of local forces! Find out more

The Devil's Brood

By Malcolm Harbrow

Christmas 1183: King Henry of England, the most powerful king in Christendom, has summoned his family together at Chinon to celebrate Christmas. The King of France will be in attendance, and the Queen has been released from prison for the occasion. But behind the celebration, Henry's empire is in crisis. The death of his eldest son has placed the succession in doubt, the Queen has been imprisoned for the last decade, his children war incessantly among themselves, and France waits greedily to regain what it has lost. An Angevin historical family drama. Find out more

Your long time friend has gone missing. He makes dolls from home but for about a month, these dolls have not been sent to buyers. You have been called, as his friend, to find out what has happened to him. An atmospheric horror set in the 1950s. Find out more

Snow White

By Shifting Forrest Storyworks, run by Scott Kelly

Of all traditional fairy tales that were adapted and censored for children in modern renditions, Snow White has perhaps undergone the most change. Today's bedtime story of an innocent princess, seven happy little men and true love forgets the original undertones of desperation, passion, poverty and sexuality. One cannot tell the true story of Snow White without going back to a time in which men were valued only for their size and stamina, and woman only for their beauty and ability to bear children; a time in which glass was so rare that a polished mirror was truly a frightening thing of magic. Find out more

The Gordian Knot

110 player space opera Saturday night. Sign up now

THE GORDIAN KNOT

By Hannah Jackson, Malu Fink, Prema Cottingham, Daphne Cohen, Joseph Parker and Joel Williams.

Our big Saturday evening larp The Gordian Knot is an original space opera. The Galactic Federation is holding a senate meeting and The Gordian Knot is set at the welcoming reception held the night before the Senate meeting. An open-invitation gathering for delegates, citizens and guests (invited or not), and the ultimate opportunity to network, politic, trade and romance.

Written for 110 players, inspirational sources for this game, and an indicator of setting and scene style include Firefly, Dr Who, Red Dwarf, Dune, Blakes 7, Star Trek, the Borg, Stargate. Babylon 5, the Apollo missions, Battlestar Galactica (new and old) and many more. However, characters are wholly original.

Step on board and the starship Kate Helva and rub shoulders amongst the stars at Chimera 2011. Find out more
