AUCKLAND AUGUST 26th-28th 2011

TIMETABLE

Friday

Chimera will kick off at 7:30pm with an informal Meet and Greet in the main hall. There will be a sign in desk where you will receive your name badge and any other important information. If you are staying on site, you will be directed to your room. The Meet and Greet is a chance to meet some of the new people and catch up with old friends before the larping kicks off. At 9pm, the first round of games will begin. Friday will wrap up at midnight.

Saturday

Breakfast will be available from 8:30am on site and no earlier. The second round of games will start at 10am sharp, all players and GMs will need to muster at the registration table at 9:50am. The games will run until 1pm, at which point there will be a one hour break for costume change and lunch. Lunch will be available on site. The third round will start at 2pm and all players and GMs are required to muster at 1:50pm. This round comes to a finish at 5pm. Dinner will be available on site until 6:30pm. Briefing for round four, The Gordian Knot, will start at 7:30pm and the game proper will begin at 8pm. The evening, complete with clean up and debrief, is expected to wind up at midnight.

Sunday

Breakfast will be available from 8:30am on site and no earlier. The fifth round of games will start at 10am sharp, all players and GMs will need to muster at the registration table at 9:50am. The games will run until 1pm, at which point there will be a one hour break for costume change and lunch. Lunch will be available on site. The sixth round will start at 2pm and all players and GMs are required to muster at 1:50pm. This round comes to a finish at 5pm. Everyone is expected to help clean up the venue and we expect to be ready to leave at 6pm. There is a short prize giving before the end of the event.
