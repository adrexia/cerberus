What is a 'lucky dip pass'?

For a reduced Chimera weekend attendance price of $25, you do not make any game selections and are put into games two weeks before the convention, to fill vital casting gaps. You could end up playing anyone in anything! It's the ultimate roleplaying and costume challenge. There are a maximum of 10 lucky dip places available.

Costs

Chimera will cost as follows:

  • Full Convention - $55
  • Friday and Saturday - $35 (no discounts apply)
  • Saturday Only - $30 (no discounts apply)
  • Sunday Only - $25 (no discounts apply)
  • One game only - $15 (no discounts apply)

Prepaid catering - $30 if you pay before August 1st.

Discounts

If you take the "Full Convention" option there is a $5 discount if you pay before August 1st, and a further $5 discount if you are a member of nzLARPS.

Bed Space

There are 84 beds available at the venue. Accommodation is clean scout bunks, with access to bathroom, shower and kitchen facilities. Accommodation is free of charge but priority will be given to those coming from out of Auckland.
