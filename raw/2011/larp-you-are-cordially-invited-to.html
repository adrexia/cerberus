You are cordially invited to...

By Paul Wilson and Jenni Dowsett

Characters

Cast List:

  • Kelly Cassidy aka Ultima - Bride. After her parents were killed in a tragic accident, she formed The Misfits with a number of other teen heroes. The Misfits have since broken up and the surviving members have been absorbed into The League of Heroes. Female
  • Jack Reynolds secretly known as Thunderbird - Groom - By day a mild mannered accountant for Reynolds, Stirling and Hellsing but when trouble calls, Thunderbird rises. Serves as the moral compass of The League of Heroes. Male
  • Dr Melissa Reynolds - Mother of the Groom - A successful psychologist and friendly ear for all of Stillwater's masked community. Female
  • Harry Reynolds - Father of the Groom - A successful real estate agent who has used his connections with the superhero community to make himself an important part of the real estate game in Stillwater and outlying areas. Male
  • Charlotte Hall - Bridesmaid. A teacher at the meta-human academy for youngsters. Sister to Jack, married to Adam. Female
  • Adam Hall - Groomsman. Assistant DA, estranged from his family but has been embraced by the Reynolds's after he married Charlotte. Male
  • Justin Malone - Best Man. A solider. Best friend of the groom and is the Government's liaison to the Legion of Heroes. Male
  • Tristram Sterling - Good friend of bride and groom. Works as an actuary, partner in Reynolds, Stirling and Hellsing firm. Male
  • Reynard Hackett secretly known as Raptor, "the bird of prey" with no apparent powers, uses violence and fear to subdue the criminal element. Looking at passing on the mantle. Male
  • Burt Hackett secretly known as Raven. Formally Sparrow, sidekick to Raptor and is Raptor's adopted son. He has grown up and established himself as a hero in his own right. Former member of The Misfits. Male
  • Chris Gibson secretly known as Sparrow - Keen and interested in taking over as the next Raptor. His parents are still alive. Male
  • Katy Hawkens secretly known as Kitty Hawk, a teen superhero who sometimes collaborates with Raptor and Sparrow. Female
  • E. Thomas Tudor - Friend of Harry Reynolds. A former Pulitzer prize winning journalist who lost his only daughter to collateral damage from Superhero fights. Has a known vendetta against Fenris. Male
  • Sarah Smith, Wedding planner. Stillwater's best wedding planner, she's just there to make sure nothing goes wrong. Female
  • Robyn Hunter, Celebrity photographer, is known for their spreads of celebrities and superheroes, specializing in weddings, newborns and breakups. Either gender
  • Gwen Michaels - Private Eye with a chequered past. Female
  • Detective Bud Stone - Stillwater P.D.'s liaison with the dark avenger, Raptor. A grizzled veteran cop who knows the city's dark secrets. Either Gender
  • Dan Christian - Fresh out of the Police Academy, Dan was partnered with Det. Bud Stone. A by the book officer, he finds Bud's approach to policing hard to swallow. Either Gender
  • Michelle Madison - Stillwater's District Attorney, calm and professional, often serving as a lawyer to the League of Heroes. Female
  • Edgar Edmundsen aka Eldritch - A trusted source of supernatural information and a practitioner of magick. Before his retirement he was a cornerstone of The League. Male
  • Cassandra Wheeler. Trusted assistant to Edgar Eldritch and expert on the occult. Cassandra is a new face to Stillwater's masked community. Female
  • Valerie Ronson - Valeria the Magnificent. Stage magician type with real magical powers, member of The League. Female
  • Lew Mulholland - Building manager, in charge of the venue. Here to make sure that these unruly hero types don't cause too much damage. Either Gender
  • Murdoch Gevaudan aka Fenris - It's hard to be a hero when you are being ridden by the spirit of the Devourer Wolf isn't easy. Fenris has used this destructive power for good. Long time member of The League and close friends with the Bride. Male
  • Billy Andrews aka The Jester, Fun loving and carefree The Jester and his partner Cyborg solve crimes (with age appropriate plots). Former member of The Misfits. Male
  • Kara 6582 - aka Cyborg, the maid of honour - Straight faced counterpart to Jester. Reliable and caring she has found her way as moral compass to Stillwater's younger heroes. Female
  • Joe Hall - A friend of the Groom's family. Seeking out his wayward brother. Either gender
  • Lisa Braden - Wife of Joe Hall dragged along by Joe Hall, He needs her for some big reveal. Female
  • Ichiro Yakamoto secretly known as Tetsujin. As head of the council of Heroes in Japan, he is here as a goodwill ambassador to his American colleague. He has fought in many epic battles alongside The League including The Mirror Wars. Former member of the Misfits. Male
  • Lord Mycroft-Bingley aka M - Victorian gentleman gorilla. Previously of The League, but now mostly retired. Currently serving as Her Majesty's Ambassador Pleneipotentairy to the Americas. Male
  • Captain Jean De Monfort, sometime associate of The League. Captain Jean defends the airways with his sky ship The Lady Destiny. Male

Costume

Formal and Superhero costume (either or neither)
