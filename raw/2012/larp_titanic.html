Background

11.10pm, 14th April 1912.

The RMS Titanic slips through the chill dark silken waters of the Atlantic Ocean. Silence reigns all around, but on board the rich and famous are making merry while the poor and hopeful celebrate in their own unique way. Laughter burbles forth, tinkling like ice on crystal while music and the low murmur of voices weaves a sturdy human tapestry the speeds through this bitter alien seascape, in which no human is meant to exist. 3 hours and it will seem to be their whole world, or their grave...

Join us for the first and last voyage of the RMS Titanic. A one-off drama that has been written to commemorate the centenary anniversary of the momentous accident that made the entire world stop to draw breath.
