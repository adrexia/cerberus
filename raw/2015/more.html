The Venue

Chimera will run at Motu Moana Scout Camp, in Green Bay, Auckland. It is located in Connaught Street and is 3km from New Lynn Transport Centre and Lynn Mall.

Catering on site

Meals for Saturday and Sunday are available on site. Breakfast will be $6 and dinners and lunches will be $8 per meal. Soft drinks and candy will be available also for a gold coin cost.

It costs $35 to pre-order meals for the whole weekend. Pre-payments need to be in by August 1st so we can pass numbers to our cook. Catering payments are separate to the convention cost. We apologise for the rise in costs, this is due to rising food prices.

Gluten free, vegetarian and vegan options are available. Please be sure to fill in your requirements on your registration form.

Our caterer for this year is Michelle Burt, the regular caterer for our sister convention, Hydra! Menu for 2014 to follow shortly.

Prizes and the Wall of Awesome

Prizes are being phased out at Chimera. This year, voting will take place only on game writing for original games to continue to celebrate excellence in game writing.

Chimera continues to encourage the open support of each other's awesomeness across the board - great roleplaying moments of all kinds, cool costumes whether it's something flashy or a really well thought out ensemble of small touches and the many helping hands that make our experiences great. We'd like to encourage you all to contribute often to our Wall of Awesome. We'll have pens and post-its available and we want you to write down and stick up all the really cool things that you see others do, all those moments that make your game. The wall isn't about the 'best' or any sort of competition, it's about a celebration of the abundance of awesome we have.

For our Saturday night flagships, for each of the two games the GMs will select one player for Game Masters' Choice for Outstanding Roleplaying, as well as Game Masters' Choice for Outstanding Costuming. Players will vote after the game paper forms, on the following three categories:

  • Favourite Male Costume
  • Favourite Female Costume
  • Memorable Roleplayer (you can nominate up to two)

There will be character name/real name lists to assist with the voting process.

Accommodation

There are 84 beds available at the venue. Accommodation is clean scout bunks, with access to bathroom, shower and kitchen facilities. Accommodation is free of charge but priority will be given for those coming from out of Auckland.

Code of Conduct

The Chimera organisational team would like Chimera to be a safe and fun event for all attendees. For this reason, we have adopted a Code of Conduct for all participants to follow. It was created to protect the safety, well-being and comfort of all our attendees.

Chiefly, we ask that you take responsibility for yourself, look after yourself and others, be responsible with alcohol and do everything reasonably in your power to look out for the enjoyment of others at the event.

Please make sure you read it before you register for the event: Chimera Code of Conduct.
