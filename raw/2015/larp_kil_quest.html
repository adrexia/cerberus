KiL Quest III: Rock Music

Author

By Te Kahurangi Patuwai-Findlay

Background

Calling all mercenaries. The town of High Point in the Duchy of Lionmane in the Kingdom of Infinite Light is willing to hire any who call themselves heroes to find our lost Mage. You will receive your instructions from the town of High Point, and then proceed to the forest where they were last seen. May Jah protect you (But a sword and armour wouldn’t hurt).

A fantasy adventure suitable for beginners.

Character Classes

  • Tinker: These people are the Artisans, Alchemists, Mechanics and Blacksmiths. They fix things, or invent them.
  • Healer: These people are the Priests, Druids, Herbalists and Wise-men. They heal people.
  • Fighter: These people are the Soldiers, Knights, and Duelists. They hurt people.
  • Ranger: These people are the Bowmen, Scouts and Explorers. They find things… and people!
  • Bard: These people are the Poets, Musicians and Storytellers. They record events and sing songs of brave adventurers. They also have knowledge that can prove helpful.
  • Mage: These people are the Wizards, Mystics and Sorcerers. They may help people...or hurt them!
  • + ComBot (if you are an adventurous costume builder) ComBots provide heavy hitting firepower to the group. Requires a Tinker to repair.


Characters

4-7 adventurers, 7 crew.

Costume

General Fantasy. Steampunk for ‘Bots. Larp safe boffer weapons, as this is a combat larp.
